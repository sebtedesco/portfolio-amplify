About Me

Hey there! My name is Sebastian Tedesco and I'm a Technical Implementation Engineer based in Louisville, CO. I quickly discovered my passion for technology as a student at the University of Colorado at Boulder and so I obtained a Certificate in Design Technologies through the College of Engineering and Applied Science.

Upon graduation, I enrolled in an accelerated Web Development program where I was emmersed in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript (ES5/ES6+), React, and Node.js. I'm also proficient using version control systems (Git/GitHub), planning resources (Figma, MeisterTask and Trello) as well as database technologies, such as PostgreSQL.

I completed a UI Engineering internship at MK Decision and later joined the team full-time as a Quality Assurance Engineer. MK Decision (acquired by Alkami Technology) is a loan origination platform that provides technology solutions for lenders to streamline the loan application and approval process. As a Quality Assurance Engineer, I wrote automated scripts to perform end-to-end user interface testing. I worked closely with the Product and Engineering team to provide feedback collaborate on the product road maps.

When MK Decision was acquired by Alkami Technology in 2021, I joined the Loan Origination department as a Technical Implementation Engineer. I was the technical point of contact for clients during their implementation process, providing technical support, guidance, and QA while implementing their environments. I worked closely with DevOps, Dev, Product Managers, Project Managers, and Customer Service teams to streamline implementation processes and help improve performance and customer satisfaction.
